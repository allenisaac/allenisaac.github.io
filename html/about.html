    About Me

    My name is Isaac Allen. I'm a data visualization and data science enthusiast. After beginning my college career studying illustration, I quickly realized I needed something more quantitative. This realization brought me to Boston University, where I recently received my Bachelors of Arts in Mathematics and Statistics. While there I garnered a love for everything data. I've continued to use my artistic background to help me find more creative solutions, while also influencing my presentations and visualizations. Although I'm now out of school, I continue learning whenever I can; whether it be research for a new personal project, or additional programming skills and techniques, I'm always trying to push the boundaries of my knowledge.

    I'm currently searching for work in data focused roles; whether it be data analysis, science, or visualization, I would like to continue to learn. I'm always trying to find new ways to analyze and use data, and I would be inclined to do so in my work. In my free time I work on various personal projects from creating artificial neural networks to painting, and I try to hike whenever I can.

    Please feel free to contact me with any business inquiries, comments, or questions.
